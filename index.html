    ABOUT US

    Welcome to DesignSphere, where the perfect blend of artistry and functionality brings your dream spaces to vibrant life.

    We've been established since 2005.Our impressive journey is defined by more than a decade of hands-on experience, during which we've consistently provided outstanding solutions that have redefined the benchmarks for quality and sustainability in the contracting industry.
    we believe that your home or workspace should be a reflection of your personality, a sanctuary of comfort, and a hub of inspiration. Our vision is to transform your vision into reality by creating interior spacesthat not only captivate the eye but also elevate your quality of life.Our company excels in a diverse range of services, catering to the world of office interiors and installations. Our expertise covers civil interiors, procurement of furniture, and modular workstations.

    At the heart of our success is our skilled and creative team, consisting of carpenters, painters, electricians, technicians, and supervisors. They are the true artisans who consistently deliver exceptional results. Their unique creativity and innovative approaches ensure that every project is a masterpiece.
